## EXPECTATIONS FOR REDROOMREWARDS

#### Promotion Design Expectations
- Ensure visuals are enticing yet align with the overall brand guideline.
- Include clearly defined calls to action to maximize conversions.
- Adapt promotions to various platforms (mobile, desktop, etc.) without losing clarity.

#### Payload Formatting
- Follow JSON structure strictly to maintain consistent integrations.
- Include mandatory fields such as `reward_id`, `user_metadata`, and `timestamp`.
- Document all optional fields and ensure backward compatibility.

#### Boundary Clarifications
- Restrict reward integration to authorized sections only.
- Respect user privacy policies and ensure data is securely transmitted.
- Avoid cross-promotions with unrelated campaigns to ensure messaging clarity.

#### Reliability Rules
- Ensure the RedRoomRewards system is monitored for 99.9% uptime.
- Implement fallback reward systems for edge cases where the main system could fail.
- Log and report anomalies in reward assignment within the data pipeline.
